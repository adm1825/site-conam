Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy is important to us. Conam Tech Services LLC respects your privacy regarding any information we may collect through our portfolio of applications (including, but not limited to, Helper and HelperClean).

Target Audience: Our applications are designed strictly for Business-to-Business (B2B) use, specifically for employers, contractors, and facility managers looking to hire workforce. They are not intended for consumer use.

Location Use: For the core functioning of connecting contractors and helpers in the same region, we request access to device location data. This is used exclusively to display available professionals within the defined distance radius.

Profile Data: User information, work history, and reviews are visible to other platform users to establish professional trust.

To request the deletion of your account or data from any of our apps, contact us via the email provided in the support section.
